Wireless mesh technology

Our state-of-the art wireless mesh protocol coupled with smart algorithms deliver unparalled battery life for your devices. The technology can be seamlessly deployed on the hardware of your choice, especially on cost-effective, low-power systems. This flexibility is powered by extremely portable software which ensures optimal performance and broad compatibility across a wide range of devices.
